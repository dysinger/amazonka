amazonka-importexport-0.0.1.0: AWS Import/Export SDK

AWS Import/Export accelerates moving large amounts of data into and out

of AWS using portable storage devices for transport. AWS transfers your

data directly onto and off of storage devices using Amazon’s high-speed

internal network and bypassing the Internet. For significant data sets,

AWS Import/Export is often faster than Internet transfer and more cost

effective than upgrading your connectivity.
