amazonka-sqs-0.0.1.0: Amazon Simple Queue Service SDK

Amazon Simple Queue Service (SQS) is a fast, reliable, scalable, fully

managed message queuing service. SQS makes it simple and cost-effective

to decouple the components of a cloud application. You can use SQS to

transmit any volume of data, at any level of throughput, without losing

messages or requiring other services to be always available. With SQS,

you can offload the administrative burden of operating and scaling a

highly available messaging cluster, while paying a low price for only

what you use.
