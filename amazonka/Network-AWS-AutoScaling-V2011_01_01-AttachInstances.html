Network.AWS.AutoScaling.V2011_01_01.AttachInstances

Description

Attaches one or more Amazon EC2 instances to an existing Auto Scaling group. After the instance(s) is attached, it becomes a part of the Auto Scaling group. For more information, see Attach Amazon EC2 Instance(s) to Your Existing Auto Scaling Group in the Auto Scaling Developer Guide.
